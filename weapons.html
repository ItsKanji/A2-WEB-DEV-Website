    A powerful shotgun that
    launches ricocheting

    An Incarnon pistol with
    extremely high damage potential. Builds up
    Incarnon charge and
    transforms into a
    powerful beam weapon.

    A musical melee weapon
    (based on the Duviri
    Paradox theme) that creates
    resonating AoE pulses. Great for
    crowd control.

  • NATARUK
    A Sentient bow
    introduced in The New War.
    Has a unique
    perfect-release mechanic
    that boosts damage and
    critical hits.

    GRIMOIRE
    A unique secondary shaped like a floating
    book that fires homing energy projectiles.
    Stylish and effective.

    PARACESIS
    A powerful greatsword
    with Sentient-slaying
    bonuses and increasing mod
    capacity with each rank-up
    to level 40.

  • FULMIN
    A hybrid rifle that can
    switch between silent
    semi-auto mode and a
    full-auto electric burst
    mode. Ideal for versatility.

    EPITAPH
    A wrist-mounted gun used by Sevagoth. Fires
    charged shots for high damage
    or uncharged
    shots to apply status effects.

    NIKANA
    A traditional Japanese
    katana-style weapon
    with fast attacks and
    high slash damage.
