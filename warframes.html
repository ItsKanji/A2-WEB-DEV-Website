    VOLT
    A fast-moving Warframe with strong
    electric abilities and team support
    potential.

    ABILITIES
    Reap – Sends out his Shadow to inflict damage over time on enemies.
    Sow– Infests enemies to explode on death, dealing AoE damage.
    Gloom – Emits a slowing aura that also heals Sevagoth when enemies are
    damaged.
    Exalted Shadow – Unleashes his inner shadow as a second controllable form
    with its own melee abilities.



    ABILITIES
    Slash Dash – Dashes through enemies with a flurry of slashes.
    Radial Blind – Blinds enemies in a radius, opening them to finishers.
    Radial Javelin – Launches ethereal javelins that impale enemies.
    Exalted Blade – Summons a powerful energy sword with ranged slashes.





    ABILITIES
    Reservoirs – Deploys motes that grant healing, speed, or shock effects.
    Wil-O-Wisp – Sends out a spectral clone to distract enemies.
    Breach Surge – Teleports to a location and blinds enemies, causing additional
    damage from attacks.
    Sol Gate – Opens a massive solar beam to melt enemies in front of her.





    ABILITIES
    Rhino Charge – Charges forward, knocking enemies aside.
    Iron Skin – Creates a protective armor layer that absorbs damage.
    Roar – Buffs all allies' damage output for a duration.
    Rhino Stomp – Sends out a shockwave that suspends enemies in the air.





    ABILITIES
    Shock – Launches a bolt of electricity that chains between enemies.
    Speed – Increases movement and melee speed for Volt and allies.
    Electric Shield – Deploys a shield that blocks bullets and amplifies electric
    damage.
    Discharge – Electrocutes all nearby enemies, dealing AoE damage and stunning
    them.
