  • BEGINNER TIPS
  • Starting Warframe can feel overwhelming, but here are some focused tips to help you get going without confusion:
  • 1. Choose the right Warframe

    At the beginning, you'll choose between Excalibur, Mag, and Volt.

    • Excalibur – Great for balanced gameplay and melee focus.
    • Volt – Offers speed and team buffs, ideal for fast missions.
    • Mag – Can be strong later but has a learning curve.
  • 2. Follow the Star Chart Progression

    Clear missions node-by-node on the Star Chart. Each planet unlocks the next through junctions, which have goals like completing quests or
    crafting weapons.

  • 3. Mastery Rank (MR) Matters

    MR increases your access to better weapons and Warframes. To level it up, use new weapons and Warframes—each one grants XP once, so
    variety is key

  • 4. Join a clan early

    MR increases your access to better weapons and Warframes. To level it up, use new weapons and Warframes—each one grants XP once, so
    variety is key.

  • 5. Keep Leveling and Replacing Gear

    Focus on leveling up as many weapons and Warframes as you can to gain Mastery Rank XP. Even weak gear helps you progress when leveled
    to 30.

  • MODDING TIPS
  • Mods are what make your weapons and Warframes strong in Warframe. Understanding how to use them is key to surviving higher-level missions.
  • 1. What are mods?

    Mods are upgrade cards that boost damage, health, shields, energy capacity, ability strength, and more. Every weapon, Warframe, companion,
    and Archwing has its own mod slots.

  • 2. How to equip mods

    Go to your Arsenal, select a Warframe or weapon, and hit "Upgrade." Drag mods into the available slots. Each mod costs energy (mod capacity) to
    equip.

  • 3. Mod Capacity

    Your mod capacity is equal to your item’s level (1–30). If you install a reactor (Warframe) or catalyst (weapon), it doubles the capacity (called
    "potatoing").

  • 4. Polarities and Matching

    Some mod slots have symbols (like "D" or "V"). Matching a mod’s symbol to the slot cuts its cost in half. Mismatching increases the cost.

  • 5. Upgrade mods with endo

    You can fuse Endo and credits into mods to increase their strength. A Level 0 mod might give +15% power, while the same mod at max level
    gives +90%.
